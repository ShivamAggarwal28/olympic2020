INDIAN MEDALISTS

INDIAN MEDALISTS IN TOKYO OLYMPICS

Neeraj Chopra

Place Of Birth: Panipat
Date Of Birth: 24 December 1997
Achievement: Gold In Javelin Throw

India's long and agonising wait for a first Olympic athletics gold medal was ended by Neeraj Chopra at the Tokyo Games 2020. He won the javelin title with a personal-best throw of 87.58 meters.

Saikhom Mirabai Chanu

Place Of Birth: Imphal East district
Date Of Birth: 8 August 1994
Achievement: Silver In Weightlifting

Saikhom Mirabai Chanu (born 8 August 1994) is an Indian weightlifter. She won the silver medal at the 2020 Tokyo Olympics in Women's 49 kg category. Mirabai Chanu has won the World Championships and multiple medals at the Commonwealth Games. She was awarded the Padma Shri by the Government of India for her contributions to the sport.

Ravi Kumar Dahiya

Place Of Birth: Nahri, Sonipat district, Harayana
Date Of Birth: 12 December 1997
Achievement: Silver In Wrestling

Ravi Kumar Dahiya, is an Indian freestyle wrestler who won a silver medal at the 2020 Tokyo Olympics in the 57 kg category. Dahiya is also a bronze medalist from 2019 World Wrestling Championships and a two-time Asian champion.

Men's Hockey Team

Field hockey, a sport which India has such a rich history and tradition of. A sport which has given the country as many as 11 Olympic medals, including 8 golds (1928 - 1980) has reignited its love affair on the Olympic stage with India. The Indian hockey team had registered their bronze medal at the Tokyo Olympics and overall the 12th Olympic medal won in men's field hockey by India at the Olympics.

PV Sindhu

Place Of Birth: Hyderabad
Date Of Birth: 5 July 1995
Achievement: Bronze In Badminton

Reigning world champion Sindhu, who had won a silver at the Rio Games five years ago, walked out of the Indira Gandhi International Terminal amid a round of applause from the airport staff. By winning the bronze in Tokyo Olympics, Sindhu became the first woman athlete from the country to bag two Olympic medals.

Lovlina Borgohain

Place Of Birth: Baromukhia, Barpathar Golaghat, Assam
Date Of Birth: 2 October 1997
Achievement: Bronze In Boxing

Lovlina Borgohain (born 2 October 1997) is an Indian amateur boxer who won a bronze medal at the 2020 Summer Olympics in the women's welterweight event, becoming only the third Indian boxer to win a medal at the Olympics.Borgohain is the first female athlete and the second boxer from Assam to represent the state in the Olympics. In 2020, she became the sixth person from Assam to receive Arjuna Award.
